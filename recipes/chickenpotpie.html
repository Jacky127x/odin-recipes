Chicken Pot Pie Bites

These chicken pot pie bites are super easy to make. A simple dish that even the pickiest kid would love

    Ingredients
  • 1 cup of frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 cup of shredded chicken
  • 1 can of cream of chiken soup
  • 1 can of Pilsbury cresent rolls
    Steps
  1. Heat oven to 375 degrees Farenheit
  2. mix vegetables, chicken, and soup together
  3. cut out 8 circles from the unrolled cresent dough
  4. spray muffin tin and place dough in each cup
  5. divide chicken mixture into each cup
  6. Bake 14 to 18 minutes or until mixture is heated through
  7. Enjoy!
