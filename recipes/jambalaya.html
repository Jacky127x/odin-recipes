Jambalaya

This delicious souther classic dish will having you blasting the Jazz music and booking your flight straight to the French Quarter. The hints of spice will set your sould ablaze with excitement

    Ingredients
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • Jalapeno
  • Cajun Seasoning
  • Garlic
  • Chicken
  • Shrimp
  • Andouille sausage
  • Chicken stock
  • Crushed tomato
  • White rice
  • Okra
    Steps
  1. Saute chicken and sausage
  2. Saute Veggies
  3. Add rice, liquds and sesasoings
  4. Cover and cook for 25-30 minutes
  5. add okra and Shrimp
  6. Taste and Season
